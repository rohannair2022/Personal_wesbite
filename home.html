2024

During the Deerhacks 2024 Hackathon, my teammate and I developed a project that rates websites based on six distinct features, ranging from design and accessibility to loading speed. Additionally, we created an RGB tracker that analyzes the contrast between font and background colors, offering recommendations on whether the combination is suitable.

2023-Current

I am currently working as a Data Science Project Associate with Enactus, a student-run group at the University of Toronto. Our team is developing an ASL translator app that uses 3D-CNNs and RNNs for gesture recognition. The aim is to create an application that facilitates seamless communication between ASL signers and individuals who do not know ASL.
